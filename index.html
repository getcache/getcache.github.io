Digital currency signature technology for security and ease of transaction in today's fast-paced world.

With each online transaction, each individual unit of currency is permanently assigned a signature in the form of an encrypted serial number. The serial serves as a unique identifier that can immediately be used to track currency should it be lost or stolen.

+

Fingerprint and face two-step verification through mobile device as well as asset freezing are available as extra protection. Nobody else can possibly obtain access to your funds.

With modern data storage and computing technology, billions of units of currency can be processed and stored for quick access. Transactions are completely secure - if funds is stolen, virtually eliminating all possibility of fraud.
