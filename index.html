Digitalizing currency for more security and ease of transaction in today's fast-paced world.

Serial numbers of dollar bills are collected by the bank's specialized machine and securely encrypted as data files into individual user storage accounts. The bills are then recycled by the bank. Eventually, all paper money will be phased out, saving the federal government billions of dollars annually.

+

Fingerprint and face two-step verification through mobile device ensures that only you can spend your money. Nobody else can possibly obtain access. If you lose your mobile device, no worries! Simply use a passport or government-issued ID as a substitute, and unlock your account at a bank once you recover your device.

With modern data storage and computing technology, billions of bills can be processed per individual and stored for quick access. Since each bill is assigned a serial number, transactions are secured. If your money is somehow stolen, the bills can be recovered by serial number tracking, preventing fraud.
